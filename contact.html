Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a consultation?

You can book a consultation by filling out the contact form above or calling us directly at +91 9072 531 234. We'll confirm your appointment within 24 hours.

What's the turnaround time?

Custom stitching typically takes 3-4 weeks, bridal wear 6-8 weeks, and alterations 3-7 days. Timelines can be adjusted based on the project complexity and your deadline.

Do you offer trial fittings?

Yes! We include multiple fittings in our custom stitching service. This ensures perfect fit and allows for any adjustments you might need.

What's your payment policy?

We typically require 50% advance at the time of booking and the balance before delivery. We accept all major payment methods including UPI, bank transfer, and cash.

Can I request alterations after delivery?

Absolutely! We provide free minor alterations for 6 months after delivery. For major changes, we offer lifetime support at reasonable rates.

How should I care for my Vastra pieces?

We provide detailed care instructions with every piece. Generally, hand washing or dry cleaning is recommended to maintain the quality and longevity of your custom garments.
