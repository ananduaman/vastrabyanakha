About Vastra by Anakha

Where passion meets craftsmanship

Our Story

Vastra by Anakha began as a passion project turned into a thriving custom stitching and fashion design studio. After completing my fashion design degree, I realized that the most beautiful garments aren't found in boutiques—they're created through meaningful conversations with clients who know exactly what they want.

Every stitch, every fold, every detail is an expression of that vision. I've been fortunate to dress women for their most important moments, from daily celebrations to life-changing events.

Today, Vastra stands as a testament to the belief that everyone deserves clothing that makes them feel confident, beautiful, and authentically themselves.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create bespoke, high-quality garments that celebrate individuality and boost confidence. We believe that everyone deserves clothing tailored to their body, style, and personality—not compromises.

Our Vision

To be the preferred custom stitching and design studio known for excellence, innovation, and personalized service. We aspire to make premium, handcrafted fashion accessible to everyone who values quality and individuality.
